Innovative Humanitarian Education

The Heart Clinic Foundation's mission is to increase awareness of heart disease and promote early detection and treatment by supporting clinical training in cardiology and cardiovascular surgery as well as innnovative research in the field of modern heart medicine.

The Heart Clinic Foundation investigates in digital health projects and artificial intelligence. We consult for new devices or software programs for remote monitoring, improving data management or help developing simulation models for less invasive therapies through advanced cardiac imaging and artificial intelligence (AI).

As a center of excellence in cardiology and cardiovascular surgery the Heart Clinic Zurich and the Heart Clinic Foundation educate medical professionals to improve outcomes and quality of life through symposia or workshops. In addition, the Heart Clinic Foundation awards fellowship-grants for outstanding individuals in cardiac surgery and cardiology with a focus on minimal-invasive cardiac surgery and interventional cardiology at the Heart Clinic in Switzerland.

The Heart Clinic Foundation will donate once a year to an underprivileged patient the medical cardiac treatment he needs at the Heart Clinic Zurich. In addition, the HCF is proud to present bi-annual the HCF Humanitarian Award to an outstanding individual whose life embody personal and professional integrity, generosity and strength.
